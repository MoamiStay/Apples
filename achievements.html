Introducing: Momis

Today I will introduce this little friend. Her name is Little Moamis, just "Momis" for short. She might seem a little strange to some people, but she is completely harmless. Although she's usually pretty quiet around strangers she can be surprisingly lively when she feels comfortable. No matter how awful it might sound or how funny it looks, she loves to sing, rap and/or dance along to her favorite songs to recharge and release stress.

She tries to seem tough, but she cries easily. She'll often sob to cheesy romance series or animation films for kids..

She typically finds reality a little too tiring and boring, so she will often spend most of her time inside one of the many worlds she has created for herself.

She knows it is important to take care of oneself and stay healthy. Unfortunately she doesn't like being around too many strangers and she certainly doesn't like doing anything that requires physical effort. So after a day at the gym it's pretty likely she'll need a few hours extra sleep.

RythmHive, my favourite way of killing time!

I've been playing this game since it's release on february 4th 2021. The amount of hours I spent each day playing this game the first week after release was .. well.. questionable.

I remember this song particularly well since it was sooo hard at the time. At that point we didn't even have the 5x Speed option, so it was even "easier" than in this video.

I remeber playing this song (poorly) and only dreaming of ever being able to get a perfect score. well, LOOK AT ME NOW!

I really don't feel like I've improved that much. At this point it doesn't feel like that big of a deal being able to play this song.

So that's why I wanna leave this video here as a reminder of that... Even if you don't feel like it, you are always improving. And before you know it, the thing that seemed impossible at one point is now an easy feat.
